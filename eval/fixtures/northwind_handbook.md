# Northwind Robotics — Employee Handbook

*This is a fictional document created as a test fixture for MindDrill's evaluation. All names, policies, and numbers are invented.*

## 1. Company Overview

Northwind Robotics was founded in 2016 and is headquartered in Portland, Oregon. The company builds warehouse automation robots and employs roughly 450 people. The fiscal year ends on March 31.

## 2. Working Hours and Remote Work

The standard work week is 40 hours. Core hours, when all employees must be available, are 10am to 3pm local time. Most employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Fully remote arrangements require VP approval. Distributed teams must maintain at least 4 hours of daily overlap across time zones.

## 3. Paid Time Off

Full-time employees receive 20 days of paid time off (PTO) per year, accrued monthly. Up to 5 unused PTO days may be carried over into the next year; any remaining days are forfeited. PTO requests must be submitted at least two weeks in advance through the HR portal and approved by the employee's manager.

Sick leave is separate from PTO. Employees receive 10 days of sick leave per year, and sick leave does not carry over.

## 4. Parental Leave

A primary caregiver receives 12 weeks of paid parental leave. A secondary caregiver receives 4 weeks of paid parental leave. Parental leave must be taken within 12 months of the child's birth or adoption.

## 5. Benefits

Northwind pays 80% of the health insurance premium for the employee and 60% of the premium for dependents. The company matches 100% of 401(k) contributions up to 4% of salary, and 401(k) contributions vest immediately. Employees receive a wellness stipend of $50 per month and a one-time home-office stipend of $500.

## 6. Expenses

Expenses must be submitted within 30 days of purchase through Expensify. Meals during travel are reimbursed up to $60 per day. Any expense over $500 requires director approval. Approved expenses are reimbursed within 14 days.

## 7. Security and Data Handling

Passwords must be at least 14 characters long and must be rotated every 180 days. Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is required for all internal systems. Access to production systems requires MFA, manager approval, and completed security training.

Data is classified into four levels: Public, Internal, Confidential, and Restricted. Customer data is always classified as Restricted and must never be stored on personal devices. Security incidents must be reported within 1 hour to the security team through the #security channel.

## 8. Onboarding

On their first day, new hires complete IT setup, receive a badge, and are assigned a buddy. Security training must be completed within the first two weeks. Managers hold 30-, 60-, and 90-day check-ins with new hires.

## 9. Code of Conduct

Northwind prohibits harassment of any kind. Violations may be reported to HR or through the anonymous ethics hotline. Conflicts of interest must be disclosed within 7 days.

## 10. Equipment and IT

Laptops are replaced every 3 years. Lost or stolen devices must be reported to IT immediately, and remote wipe is enabled on all company devices.

## 11. Performance and Reviews

Performance reviews are held twice a year, in April and October. The promotion cycle runs once a year, in April.
